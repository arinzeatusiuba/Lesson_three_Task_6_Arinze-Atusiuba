My Projects

Computer Based Test App – UX Case Study

This Computer Based Test Application is designed for both Examination Boards and Individuals (Students). It provides an avenue where Examination Boards can upload their exams and a platform where individuals can sit for exams from their homes, write and view their results thereafter.

Management Solution – UX Case Study

ELECTMAG App is created to give access to every Electrical Engineer who needs NEMSA (Nigerian Electricity Management Services Agency) to inspect and approve their Electricity Projects, award them professional certifications, issue them warehouse space to store their electrical appliances and be able to report electrical hazards to the body. Requesting for services through the App eases the burden of locating the branch office in every State to make a report or request for service.

Veechange: A Currency Exchange Application

This application is designed to breach the gap between 2 customers with different currency needs. For instance: Someone with US Dollar who wants Naira can exchange their Naira with someone who has Naira and wants to get US Dollars through a verified third party.
